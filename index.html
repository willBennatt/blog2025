About Me

My name is Will. On this blog, I write about experiences I've had and whether I will or won't do them again.

I like all things outdoors and often write about my climbing, skiing, or hiking trips. I lived in Seattle for almost 8 years and toured all over the Cascade mountains, but I moved to Munich, Germany, in 2025 where I am now exploring the Alps.

You might find the occasional post about the tech industry or software engineering as well — I worked at Amazon Web Services for three and a half years and enjoy coding small projects in my free time, like this website.
